ABOUT ME

I'm a full stack web developer specializing in the MERN stack. Since then, I discovered web development, which allows me to combine two of my passions, graphic design and programming. I've largely taught myself via resources such as Codeacademy, Free Code Camp (where I've obtained certifications in Responsive Web Design, JavaScript Algorithms and Data Structures, Front End Libraries, and APIs and Microservices), and, of course, StackOverflow and Google. I've also sought to complement this with traditional CS classes at my local community college and online coding competitions on Hackerrank and CodeSignal in order to strengthen my CS fundamentals.

I love how programming empowers people to solve problems and create new products and solutions with (relatively) minimal investment. I'm always on the lookout for new opportunities to advance my career and skills, and love to learn new technologies and topics. For more details, feel free to browse my resume.
